Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our manufacturing services and processes.

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

+

Our minimum order quantity varies by product type and customization level. For standard designs, the MOQ is typically 100-500 pieces. For fully custom designs, the MOQ starts at 500-1000 pieces. We can discuss flexible options for new partnerships.

How long does the production process take?

+

Production timelines depend on order complexity and quantity. Sample development takes 7-10 days, while bulk production typically requires 15-30 days after sample approval. Rush orders can be accommodated with additional coordination.

Do you provide samples before bulk production?

+

Yes, we always provide samples for approval before starting bulk production. Sample costs vary based on complexity but are typically $50-200 per design. Sample fees are often refunded with confirmed bulk orders.

What customization options do you offer?

+

We offer comprehensive customization including materials, colors, sizes, logo printing/embroidery, hardware selection, interior layouts, and special features. Our design team can work with your specifications or help develop new concepts.

What is your production capacity?

+

Production Capacity: 200,000 pieces per month. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is equipped to handle large-scale orders while maintaining consistent quality standards.

What are your payment terms?

+

Standard payment terms are 30% deposit upon order confirmation and 70% balance before shipment. We accept T/T (wire transfer), L/C, and other secure payment methods. Terms can be negotiated for established partnerships.

Do you handle international shipping?

+

Yes, we ship worldwide and work with reliable logistics partners to ensure safe delivery. We can arrange shipping via sea freight, air freight, or express courier depending on your timeline and budget requirements.
